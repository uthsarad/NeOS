# Risk & Priority Report

## Current Risk Posture
The immediate risk of build-blocking failures has been mitigated with the resolution of the `pacman.conf` and ISO validation issues. However, medium-term risks persist due to incomplete architectural documentation (ADRs) and pending improvements to error handling and documentation consistency. The primary risk is technical debt accumulation if feature development resumes before these foundational tasks are completed.

## Priority Assessment
The highest priority is maintaining the stability achieved by the recent critical fixes. The current state demands a cessation of active development to ensure the system does not drift from its product goals and remains stable for the upcoming beta release.
